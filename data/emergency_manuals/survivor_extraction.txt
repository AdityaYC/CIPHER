Survivor extraction procedures.

Triage: Use START (Simple Triage and Rapid Treatment). RPM â€” Respiration, Perfusion, Mental status. Priority order: Immediate (red), Delayed (yellow), Minor (green), Deceased (black). Allocate resources to Immediate first; do not spend time on deceased.

Extraction: Stabilize spine if mechanism suggests injury. Use minimal movement. Create space (cribbing, lifting) rather than pulling. Protect airway during move. Document condition before and after move.
